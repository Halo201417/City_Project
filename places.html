Places of Interest

Valladolid is a city rich in architecture, nature, and culture. Here are some of the essential places you must visit to capture the essence of the city and its surroundings.

Cavalry Academy

One of the most iconic and photographed buildings in the city. Its spectacular eclectic architecture, reflected in the waters of the Pisuerga river, is a must-see.

Cathedral of Valladolid

Know as "The Unfinished", this impressive Herrerian-style building was designed by Juan de Herrera. Don't miss the chance to climb its tower for panoramic views.

Campo Grande Park

The city's green lung. This romantic 19th century park is perfect for a stroll, with its ponds, waterfalls, and resident peacocks roaming freely.

Ribera dle Duero Vineyards

Just a short drive from the city, you'll find the prestigious Ribera del Duero wine region. Explore historic wineries (bodegas) and taste world-class wines.
