Getting Around

Valladolid is a well-connected city. Whether by bus, train, or foot, moving around is easy and efficient.

Urban Transport

The local bus network is the best way to reach every corner of the city.

AUVASA Buses

Recognizable by their colors, they connect neighborhoods efficiently. You can pay only with card.

High Speed Train (AVE)

Valladolid is a major hub for high-speed trains in Spain.

Valladolid Campo Grande Station

Located in the city center, it connects Valladolid with the Spanish railway network.
