Sercives for Visitors & Locals

Whether you are visiting or live here, Valladolid offers world-class services to make your stay perfect. From its renowned gastronomy to comfortable accommodation.

Gastronomy: The Taste of Valladolid

Valladolid is famous for its "tapas" and its hearty "lechazo" (roast lamb). The city is the perfect place to explore the flavour of Castile and León.

Featured Dish: Lechazo

This tender, wood-oven-roasted lamb is a local delicacy you must try.
